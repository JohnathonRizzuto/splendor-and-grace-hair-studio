About the Studio
A small, intentional studio in downtown Sandwich.

Splendor and Grace is the kind of place where you sit down, you're handed coffee, and you actually get to talk about what you want before scissors come anywhere near your hair.

We work in small batches, by appointment, in a calm space — no rushed walk-ins, no shouting over six other dryers, no upselling you on services you didn't ask about. Just thoughtful cuts and color, in a studio that feels more like someone's living room than a chain salon.

If you've been somewhere that just didn't get it — try us once. The first consultation is always free.

— the team
